## Blog: The Rise of Autonomous AI Agents

Autonomous AI agents are moving beyond simple prompt-response patterns into systems that independently plan, execute, and iterate on tasks. Their architecture — perception, planning, memory, tool-use, and self-reflection — mirrors microservice design, and that parallel has been fundamental to scaling the field. Multi-agent coordination, event-driven orchestration, and standardized protocols like MCP and A2A are making production-grade agent systems a reality in 2026.
